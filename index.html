About

Before I became a UW Bootcamp learner, I was a community manager at 7 Generation Games. For two years, my focus had centered on expanding the reach of 7 Generation Games and its federally funded project, Growing Math. This project delivered teacher training webinars, digital lesson resources and math games at no cost. Growing Math homed in on six U.S. states with Indigenous and Latino populations. I was previously a middle school educator for three years.
